RPMFusion e paket za install na razlichni paketi,koito standartno ne se vkliuchvat v paketite za RedHat distribucii.

Installing RPMFusion:

sudo rpm -Uvh http://download1.rpmfusion.org/free/fedora/rpmfusion-free-release-stable.noarch.rpm http://download1.rpmfusion.org/nonfree/fedora/rpmfusion-nonfree-release-stable.noarch.rpm


RPMFusion - fedora 20-1 x86-64:
rpm -Uvh http://download1.rpmfusion.org/free/fedora/releases/20/Everything/x86_64/os/rpmfusion-free-release-20-1.noarch.rpm

rpm -Uvh http://download1.rpmfusion.org/nonfree/fedora/releases/20/Everything/x86_64/os/rpmfusion-nonfree-release-20-1.noarch.rpm
